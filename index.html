About Carolina Valet Waste

We’re a locally owned valet waste management company based in North Carolina. Because we genuinely care about our community, we go above and beyond simply hauling tenants’ trash to communal dumpsters. Our team also collects your tenants’ weekly recyclables to haul them off-site. We want to make it as easy and convenient as possible for you, as a property owner or manager, while your tenants enjoy a cleaner and more sustainable environment.

Community Awareness


We pride ourselves on how much we do for our North Carolina community. We’re actively working towards increasing the number of households and apartment complexes that recycle as much as they can. We’re taking the time to educate tenants and property managers on the best waste management and recycling practices that they can use in their daily lives, and we can do the same for you.

Encouraging Sustainability


Carolina Valet Waste is making progress profitable! We’re offering tenants and property managers monthly incentives to make sure that they not only participate in creating a greener community, but that they also get a greater return on their hard work and investment.

Our Range of Services

We offer a wide range of waste removal and recycling services. Each member of our service and management team is dedicated to giving you the very best in waste management. Take a look at some of the services listed below to see how we can give you the convenience, care, and professionalism you deserve.

Doorstep Trash Pickup


We’ll pick your tenants’ trash up right from their doorstep. How’s that for convenient? A uniformed trash valet will arrive after leasing hours to pick up, transport, and dispose of your commercial property waste as quickly as possible. Our doorstep trash pickup services are available from Sunday-Thursday every week.

Doorstep Recycling Pickup


Our uniformed trash valet will collect your tenants’ recyclables right from their doorstep. We want to make recycling as easy as possible, which is why our valet not only collects your residents’ recyclables, but also filters out any items that are not actually recyclable to ensure your community is recycling right. Our recycling services are available Sunday-Thursday after community leasing hours.

Doorstep Cleanup


When it comes to waste removal, we’ll go the extra sweep to make sure that no trash or waste stays behind after we’ve done our rounds. We’ll make sure your tenants’ breezeways are squeaky clean so they can enjoy a spotless and tidy doorstep.
