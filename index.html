Services

Doorstep Trash Pickup


Resident’s trash and waste will be picked up by a uniformed trash valet Sunday – Thursday after community leasing hours. Trash will then be transported to and disposed of in the community dumpster(s).

Doorstep Recycling Pickup


Resident’s recyclables will be collected up by a uniformed trash valet once a week on Thursday after community leasing hours. Recyclables will be collected and transported to an offsite recycling center.

Doorstep Cleanup


In addition to picking up resident’s trash, our team will make sure there is no excess trash that has spilled over. Residents are guaranteed a clean and trash-free breezeway.

About

We at Carolina Valet Waste take pride in the services that we offer. Not only do we collect and haul resident’s trash to your community’s dumpsters for them. We also collect and haul recyclables off-site once a week allowing your residents

to actively participate in creating a much more sustainable community. Being locally owned gives us the ability to offer competitive rates and more importantly focus on the individualized service your community requires.
